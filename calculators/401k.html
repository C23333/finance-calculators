About This Calculator

How to Use This Calculator

Enter your current age, retirement age, salary, and contribution details to see how your 401(k) will grow over time. The calculator factors in employer matching and investment returns to project your retirement balance.

The Power of Employer Matching

Employer matching is essentially free money. If your employer matches 50% of contributions up to 6% of your salary, you should contribute at least 6% to get the full match. Not doing so is leaving money on the table.

2026 Contribution Limits

  • Under 50: $23,500 employee contribution limit
  • 50 and older: Additional $7,500 catch-up contribution allowed
  • Total limit (including employer): $70,000

Investment Strategy Tips

  • Choose low-cost index funds when available
  • Diversify across stocks, bonds, and international investments
  • Consider target-date funds for automatic rebalancing
  • Review and rebalance your portfolio annually
