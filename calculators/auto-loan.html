How to Get the Best Auto Loan

Getting pre-approved for an auto loan before visiting the dealership gives you negotiating power and helps you understand your budget.

Tips for Lower Payments

  • Improve your credit score: Even a small improvement can significantly lower your rate
  • Make a larger down payment: 20% down is ideal to avoid being "underwater"
  • Choose a shorter term: You'll pay less interest overall
  • Shop around: Compare rates from banks, credit unions, and online lenders

New vs Used Car Financing

  • New cars typically have lower interest rates but higher prices
  • Used cars depreciate less but may have higher rates
  • Certified pre-owned (CPO) vehicles often offer the best of both worlds
