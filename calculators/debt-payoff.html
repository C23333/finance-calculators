Snowball vs Avalanche Method

Debt Avalanche (Mathematically Optimal)

Pay off debts with the highest interest rate first. This method saves the most money on interest over time.

Debt Snowball (Psychologically Motivating)

Pay off debts with the smallest balance first. Quick wins keep you motivated, even if you pay slightly more interest.

Which Method Should You Choose?

  • Choose Avalanche if: You're motivated by saving money and can stay disciplined
  • Choose Snowball if: You need quick wins to stay motivated, or have many small debts

Tips for Faster Debt Payoff

  • Always pay at least the minimum on all debts
  • Put all extra money toward one debt at a time
  • Consider balance transfer cards for high-interest debt
  • Avoid taking on new debt while paying off existing debt
