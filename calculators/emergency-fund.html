Why You Need an Emergency Fund

An emergency fund protects you from unexpected expenses like job loss, medical bills, or car repairs without going into debt.

How Much Should You Save?

  • 3 months: Minimum for stable income, dual-income households
  • 6 months: Recommended for most people
  • 9-12 months: Self-employed, single income, or volatile industry

Where to Keep Your Emergency Fund

  • High-yield savings account (easy access, earns interest)
  • Money market account
  • NOT in investments (too risky for emergency funds)
