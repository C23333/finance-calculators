How Extra Payments Help

Making extra payments on your loan goes directly toward your principal balance. This reduces the amount of interest you pay over time and shortens your loan term.

Strategies for Paying Off Debt Faster

  • Round Up Payments: Round your payment to the nearest $50 or $100
  • Bi-Weekly Payments: Pay half your monthly payment every two weeks (equals 13 monthly payments per year)
  • Lump Sum Payments: Apply tax refunds or bonuses to your principal
  • Refinance: If rates have dropped, refinancing could lower your interest rate

Important Considerations

  • Check if your loan has prepayment penalties
  • Ensure extra payments are applied to principal, not future payments
  • Consider building an emergency fund before aggressive debt payoff
