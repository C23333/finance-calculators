When Should You Refinance?

Refinancing makes sense when you can lower your interest rate enough to offset closing costs within a reasonable timeframe.

General Guidelines

  • Rate reduction of at least 0.5-1% is typically worthwhile
  • Plan to stay in the home long enough to reach break-even
  • Consider shortening your term to build equity faster
  • Factor in all closing costs, not just the rate
