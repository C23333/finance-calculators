About This Calculator

How to Use This Calculator

Enter the home purchase details, your current rent, and how long you plan to stay. The calculator compares the total costs of renting versus buying, including equity built and home appreciation, to help you make an informed decision.

Advantages of Buying

  • Build equity over time
  • Potential tax deductions
  • Stable monthly payments (fixed-rate mortgage)
  • Freedom to customize your home

Advantages of Renting

  • Flexibility to move easily
  • No maintenance responsibilities
  • Lower upfront costs
  • No risk of home value decline

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it better to rent or buy a home?

The answer depends on your financial situation, how long you plan to stay, local market conditions, and personal preferences. Generally, buying makes more sense if you plan to stay 5+ years.

How long should I plan to stay to make buying worthwhile?

Most financial experts suggest staying at least 5-7 years to make buying worthwhile. This allows you to build equity, offset closing costs, and benefit from potential home appreciation.

What costs are included in home ownership?

Home ownership costs include mortgage payments, property taxes, homeowners insurance, maintenance and repairs (typically 1-2% of home value annually), and HOA fees if applicable.
