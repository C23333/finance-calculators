Understanding Your Salary

Knowing how your salary breaks down across different time periods helps you budget effectively and compare job offers.

Common Pay Periods

  • Weekly: 52 paychecks per year
  • Bi-Weekly: 26 paychecks per year (every 2 weeks)
  • Semi-Monthly: 24 paychecks per year (1st and 15th)
  • Monthly: 12 paychecks per year

Salary vs Hourly

Salaried employees typically receive the same pay regardless of hours worked, while hourly employees are paid for actual hours worked and may receive overtime pay for hours over 40 per week.
