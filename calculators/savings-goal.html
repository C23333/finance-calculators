About This Calculator

How to Use This Calculator

Enter your savings goal amount, current savings, timeframe in months, and expected interest rate. The calculator will show you exactly how much you need to save each month, week, and day to reach your goal.

Automate Your Savings

Set up automatic transfers from your checking to savings account right after payday. This "pay yourself first" approach ensures you save before you spend.

Common Savings Goals

  • Emergency Fund: 3-6 months of expenses
  • Down Payment: 20% of home price
  • Vacation: Varies by destination
  • New Car: 10-20% down payment
  • Wedding: Average $30,000 in the US

Where to Keep Your Savings

  • High-Yield Savings: Best for emergency funds and short-term goals
  • CDs: Higher rates for money you won't need for a set period
  • Money Market: Combines savings with limited check-writing

Frequently Asked Questions

What interest rate should I use?

High-yield savings accounts currently offer 4-5% APY. CDs may offer slightly higher rates for longer terms.

Should I invest my savings instead?

For short-term goals (under 5 years), keep money in savings. For long-term goals, consider investing for potentially higher returns.

What if I can't save the full amount?

Start with what you can afford and increase over time. Even small amounts add up with compound interest.
