Student Loan Repayment Strategies

Federal Loan Options

  • Standard Repayment: Fixed payments over 10 years
  • Income-Driven: Payments based on income (IBR, PAYE, SAVE)
  • PSLF: Forgiveness after 10 years of public service

Tips to Pay Off Faster

  • Make payments during grace period
  • Pay more than the minimum when possible
  • Consider refinancing for lower rates (but lose federal benefits)
