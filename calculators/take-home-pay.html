About This Calculator

How to Use This Calculator

Enter your gross salary, pay frequency, filing status, and state. Add any pre-tax deductions like 401(k) contributions and health insurance. The calculator will show your net take-home pay after all taxes and deductions.

Tax Withholdings

  • Social Security: 6.2% up to $168,600

Frequently Asked Questions

What is FICA tax?

FICA (Federal Insurance Contributions Act) taxes fund Social Security and Medicare. Employees pay 6.2% for Social Security (up to $168,600) and 1.45% for Medicare.

How do pre-tax deductions help?

Pre-tax deductions like 401(k) and health insurance reduce your taxable income, lowering your tax burden and increasing your effective take-home pay.

What's the Additional Medicare Tax?

An extra 0.9% Medicare tax applies to wages over $200,000 (single) or $250,000 (married filing jointly).
